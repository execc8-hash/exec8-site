The seven non-negotiable rules

  • Maximum 1 open position at a time
  • Stop loss set as market order BEFORE entry confirmation
  • Three exits only: stop, target, or 72-hour time stop
  • Stops only move to breakeven (after halfway to target) or trail up
  • No position additions, no averaging down, one entry per setup
  • 24-hour cooling period after any loss
  • Weekly Sunday review is mandatory (even if zero trades)

⚠ You're selecting Half Kelly

The math at 1/2 Kelly:

Even with a verified 55% win rate and 2:1 R:R, this sizing produces approximately 50% maximum drawdowns over time. Math says so — it's not avoidable at this fraction.

Most traders cannot psychologically hold through a 50% account drawdown. They panic-close, abandon their system, or revenge trade their way to a blown account.

Recommended only if you have:

  • 100+ verified profitable trades on logged data
  • Edge confirmed across multiple market conditions
  • Demonstrated capacity to hold through 50% drawdowns without panic
  • Capital you can afford to lose half of without affecting your life

If you're not sure whether you meet all four, pick 1/4 Kelly. You'll compound just fine and sleep at night.
